Open Data Directive

In light of Russia's military action against Ukraine and concerns about ensuring the proper use of European funds, an "Open Data Directive" could be a valuable tool for transparency and accountability. Such a directive, if implemented, would aim to make government data and financial information related to European funds more accessible to the public and stakeholders. By following the principles of data accessibility, machine-readability, open formats, and transparent licensing, this directive would enable citizens and organizations to scrutinize how European funds are allocated and spent, ensuring that they are used in a manner consistent with their intended purposes.
